What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive flow testing, calibration, and industrial measurement services traceable to national standards.

Water Flow Testing

Comprehensive multi-meter water flow testing with state-of-the-art test benches. All setups maintain minimum 15D straight length (10D upstream & 5D downstream) ensuring stable flow conditions and precise, repeatable results.

Flow MetersMulti-Meter15D Setup

Flow Meter Calibration

Precision calibration services for all types of flow meters, including flow measurement verification and instrument accuracy validation. All calibrations are traceable to national standards ensuring complete reliability.

CalibrationNational StandardsVerification

Endurance & Life Cycle Testing

Long-duration endurance testing for water meters and industrial valves. Includes hydrostatic pressure testing and temperature suitability evaluation to validate product durability and operational life under real-world conditions.

EnduranceLife CyclePressure Testing

Temperature Suitability Evaluation

Systematic temperature suitability testing to evaluate meter and valve performance across varying temperature conditions, ensuring reliable operation in diverse environmental and industrial scenarios.

TemperatureEnvironmentalSuitability

Industrial Valve Testing

Specialized testing for industrial valves including the T-CON UNIFLOW 5-in-1 Control Valve — featuring equal flow distribution, non-return protection, meter safety, air vent function, 100% tamper-proof design, and sealing facility.

Valve TestingT-CON UNIFLOWIndustrial

Research & Development Testing

Cutting-edge R&D testing services conducted in collaboration with TECHIN – IIT Palakkad. Supporting pilot studies, patent development, and research publication for innovative flow measurement technologies and solutions.

R&DIIT PalakkadInnovation

Flow Meter Performance Evaluation

Comprehensive performance evaluation covering accuracy class verification, pressure loss assessment, repeatability analysis, and long-term operational stability testing for all categories of water meters.

PerformanceAccuracyRepeatability
